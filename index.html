Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about how mutual fund investments operate and how we safeguard your hard-earned assets.

Yes, absolutely. AuraWealth is a SEBI registered investment adviser. Your money goes directly from your bank account to the clearing house of AMFI / Asset Management Companies, not through AuraWealth's corporate bank accounts. Even in the highly unlikely event that AuraWealth ceases operations, your units are held securely in your name in the central depositories (CDSL/NSDL) and can be accessed easily from any other distributor or direct portal.
You can start investing with as little as ₹500 per month using our Systematic Investment Plan (SIP). For one-time (lumpsum) investments, the minimum amount varies by fund category, typically starting from ₹1,000 or ₹5,000.
Withdrawal is highly straightforward. Log in to your dashboard, select the fund you wish to redeem, and click "Withdraw". The money will be credited directly to your registered bank account. Debt and Liquid funds are typically credited in 1 business day, whereas equity funds take 2 to 3 business days.
AuraWealth charges zero subscription fees, zero platform fees, and zero commissions. We only distribute Direct Plan mutual funds, which have lower expense ratios compared to Regular plans distributed by agents. The only costs are standard fund management charges (Expense Ratio) levied by the AMC itself.
Taxation depends on the type of fund and holding period. Equity fund gains held for more than 1 year (Long-term Capital Gains or LTCG) are taxed at 10% on gains exceeding ₹1 Lakh in a financial year. Gains held for less than 1 year (STCG) are taxed at 15%. Debt fund gains are added to your income tax slab rates regardless of holding period.
